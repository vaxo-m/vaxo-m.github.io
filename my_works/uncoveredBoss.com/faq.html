FAQ

  • Are my ratings anonymous?
    Yes, ratings are anonymous. Even though you are signing in, it will not display any of your personal information once you post your rating.
  • Why do I need to sign in to add or rate a manager?
    We want to make sure that is a real person who is adding the manager. When rating someone, it simply helps us to try to maintain an accurate score of the Leaders and Bosses; if everyone could easily vote without signing in, it would skew the average.
  • Would my manager be contacted if I add them using the Linkedin or company email?
    Absolutely No! The reason we are asking for their Linkedin account is to help maintain accurate information and making sure the person is not added multiple times into our database.
